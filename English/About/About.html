¡Hello! I am Gonzalo Ajenjo.

Although it is pronounced "Good luck with that". Just call me Gonzo. Or Gon. Honestly I´ve accepted my fate.

  • I'm curious.

    At least enough to learn HTML and CSS just to build this website from scratch. Also to fall into 45-min TikTok rabbit holes titled “10 Facts You Didn’t Know About Octopuses.” (Yes, it’s octopuses. You're welcome.)

  • High adaptability.

    Spaniard by birth, Sydneysider by current timezone. My internal clock is confused, my wardrobe is a mess, and don’t even ask me what season it is. I just pack a raincoat and hope for the best.

  • I'm hungry.

    For opportunities. For growth. And for carbs. Especially carbs. I don’t just bring food for two. I bring ideas for three and energy for five. (And if we’re being honest, snacks for six.)

  • Not the worst sense of humor.

    Always the pun-ultimate.

  • Love to play.

    Board games, word games, video games, mind games (but like... the fun kind). I get weirdly competitive at “Rock, Paper, Scissors” which probably says more about me than it should.

  • I'm restless.

    I’m the kind of person who turns “we should do this someday” into “I made a draft, thoughts?”
